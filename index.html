ACTIVE

Active projects are actively being expanded or are actively participatory. These projects funded and no longer in development.

PARTICIPATORY

Participatory projects are projects that necessitate participation to exist. Their medium is response and their collaborators are what make these projects possible. If you see a an active project with a participatory tag look for how you can add your voice.

IN DEVELOPMENT

Projects that are in development are fully formed conceptually but not technically or financially. They represent ideas that are looking for a home or the right collaborators.

OBSERVATIONAL

Observational projects are projects that have aggregated qualitative data to be presented. While the presented data may be the formalization of participants they are no longer participatory.

ARCHIVED

Archived projects are simple projects that are complete. They are no longer active. They may have been participatory at one point but are no longer.

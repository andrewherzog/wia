The W.i.A? Institute is an inquiry into the word Art – who uses it, who benefits from it, who has defined it, and how we will use and define it in the future.


Current project:


What is Art? O que é Arte? Que es Arte?
Commissioned for Walk&Talk - Arts Festival

What is art?

Does it have to be in a museum? or a gallery? on a pedestal? in a frame? is it something you feel? something you hear? something you think? something you eat? is it an object? is it work? is it leisure? is it valuable? or worthless? is it beautiful? is it Ugly? is it useful? or useless? who makes it? who owns? is it valuable? or worthless?

What is art?
